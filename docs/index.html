Our app offers a range of features to make your bike rental experience seamless:

  • Find nearby bike stations and check how many bikes are available at each station.
  • Available on Garmin devices for easy access on the go.
  • View a detailed list of available bikes at each station.
  • Navigate to your selected station directly using our app.
  • Preview locations on Google Maps for a better overview.
  • Filter bikes by type to easily find the perfect ride for your needs.
